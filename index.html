How it works

Three steps. No bots. No agents.

01 / Search

Look up any address.

Type a street, suburb or postcode. If anyone's lived there and written about it, you'll see the home, the building (if it's an apartment) and the surrounding suburb.

02 / Read honest takes

From people who lived there.

Every review comes from someone we've verified actually lived at that address. No agents. No marketing copy. No reviews of individual landlords or neighbours.

03 / Pass it on

Help the next person.

Moved out recently? Spend five minutes writing what you wish you'd known. It stays public under your first name and last initial. The address stays public so the next renter can find it.
